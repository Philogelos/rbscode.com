Scratch Missions

Drag. Drop. Dream Big.

Scratch is a kid-friendly coding platform where creativity meets logic. Instead of typing code, kids snap together colorful blocks to make games, stories, and animations.

It’s the perfect first step into the world of programming — visual, fun, and packed with imagination!

Example Game: Catch the Apple

Move the basket with the arrow keys to catch the falling apples! This simple game teaches loops, motion, sensing, and variables — all through colorful coding blocks.
