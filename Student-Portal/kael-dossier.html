CLASSIFIED FILE // ACCESS LEVEL 5

Kael Arden

Shadow Division Operative

Background

Kael grew up in the frozen provinces of the Northern Districts, where long winters and abandoned industrial zones taught him how to move quietly and stay hidden. From a young age, he trained in stealth, cold-survival, climbing, and quick infiltration. He later joined the Shadow Division, a special unit known for silent operations and dangerous recovery missions.

Core Skills

  • Silent Step — Moves across any surface without sound.
  • Shadow Cloak — Blends into darkness and low-light areas.
  • Tech Sense — Detects hidden devices, traps, and energy sources.
  • Quick Disable — Fast takedowns of machines or hostile units.

Current Mission

A powerful Deep-Core Energy Cell was stolen from a research station near the border colonies. The cell is highly unstable: if damaged, it could release enough energy to shut down an entire power grid or cause a massive explosion.

Security cameras caught a masked figure escaping into the ice tunnels moments after the theft. The tunnels are cold, narrow, and full of old machinery—perfect for hiding something dangerous.

Mission Objective: Track the thief, locate the missing energy cell, and bring it back safely before it overloads.

Threat Level

The stolen energy cell is unstable and extremely dangerous. If it is tampered with or accidentally dropped, it could trigger a chain reaction in the colony’s power systems. Kael must act fast, move silently, and avoid any fights that could shake or damage the cell once found.
