Activity 1: Build Your Own HTML Card

Fun Offline Practice Pack

This pack helps you practise real HTML by looking at examples and then creating your own hero card.

Example 1: Kael Arden Card (Result)

Here is a finished card. This is what we want the browser to show.

Kael Arden

Shadow Division Operative

Silent infiltration specialist from the northern districts.
Moves unseen. Strikes without a trace.

Core Stats:

  • Awareness: 92
  • Stealth: 97
  • Tech Skill: 89

Example 1: Kael Arden Card (HTML Code)

This is the HTML that creates the Kael Arden card.

<img width="200" src="kael-arden.png" alt="Hero">

<h1>Kael Arden</h1>
<h3>Shadow Division Operative</h3>

<hr>

<p>
  Silent infiltration specialist from the northern districts.<br>
  Moves unseen. Strikes without a trace.
</p>

<p>Core Stats:</p>

<ul>
  <li>Awareness: 92</li>
  <li>Stealth: 97</li>
  <li>Tech Skill: 89</li>
</ul>

<button>Choose This Hero</button>
